Bacon and Eggs

Home

Description

This is the perfect protein-filled breakfast you need to start your day. The bacon is crispy. The eggs are gooey. It's everything you could want to fuel yourself for the action ahead.

Ingredients

  • two eggs
  • three strips of bacon

Steps

  1. Turn skillet to medium heat
  2. Put bacon on skillet
  3. Cook bacon until crispy then remove from skillet
  4. Crack eggs and add them to the skillet
  5. Once cooked to your liking on one side, flip eggs
  6. Remove eggs and enjoy!
