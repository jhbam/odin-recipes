Peanut Butter and Jelly

Home

Description

This delicious peanut butter and jelly sandwich will remind you of the one your mother used to make. It's perfect for a delicious, quick lunch on a busy day. This is as simple and rewarding as food preparation can be.

Ingredients

  • peanut butter
  • jelly
  • two slices of bread

Steps

  1. Place the two pieces of bread on a paper plate
  2. Use a knife to spread peanut butter evenly over one of the pieces
  3. Use a knife to spread jelly evenly over the other piece
  4. Put the two pieces together with the peanut butter and jelly in the middle
  5. Cut into even halves
